# Investor Showcase: Product Overview & Core Capabilities

FedSOC AI transforms collaborative cybersecurity operations by enabling private, cross-organizational machine learning models.

---

## 1. Product Capabilities

1. **Decentralized Threat Classification**: Organizations train security threat classifiers locally inside their firewall.
2. **Federated Aggregation**: The central coordinator aggregates parameters using `FedAvg` without viewing individual training rows.
3. **AI SOC Analyst Workspace**: An AI agent running local LLMs (Llama 3.1) evaluates detections and converts them into structured incident summaries.
4. **MITRE ATT&CK Mapping**: Incidents are automatically cross-referenced with Unified Cyber Kill Chain phases.
5. **Traceable Model Governance**: Historical checkpoints log the model architecture, prompt templates, and AI evaluator scores.

---

## 2. Target Showcase Markets

- **Banking & Finance**: Share fraud and DDoS threat variables privately.
- **Healthcare**: Collaborative malware containment while maintaining HIPAA patient data privacy.
- **Critical Infrastructure**: Secure cross-utility scanning detection loops.
